Adding an e-mail attribute

On this page you can add an e-mail address as a new attribute to your IRMA app. This can be done multiple times. It works via the following three steps.

  1. Fill in your e-mail address below, and click the 'Add address' button.
  2. An e-mail message will then be sent to this address with a verification link. Click this link in your e-mail reader.
  3. Scan the resulting QR-code with your IRMA-app.
