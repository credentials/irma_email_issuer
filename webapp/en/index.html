Adding an e-mail address

On this page you can add an e-mail address to your IRMA app. If you want to add multiple e-mail addresses to the IRMA app, you have to perform these steps multiple times.

Please check your e-mail address again for correctness.

It may take a couple of minutes for the e-mail to arrive. If you do not see it, check your spam folder too. Please go through the following steps:

  1. Open your e-mail application.
  2. Open the e-mail message sent by Privacy by Design Foundation.
  3. Open the link within the e-mail message.
  4. Go through the steps that are offered.
